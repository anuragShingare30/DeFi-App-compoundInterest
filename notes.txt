// WHAT ARE CYCLES AND ICP TOKENS.

1. TO DEPLOY OUR WEB APP ON IC WE NEED SOME ICP TOKENS.
2. BY COINBASE, DFINITY, NEURAL NETWORK SYSTEM(NNS) AND ACTING AS DATA CENTER, WE CAN GET SOME ICP TOKENS.
3. "ONCE WE GET AN ICP TOKENS, WE CAN BURN THEM AND EXCHANGE THEM FOR COMPUTATIONAL CYCLES IN OUR CANISTER."
4. THIS CYCLES ALLOWS US TO STORE DATA AND ALLOWS US TO PERFORM COMPUTATIONS IN OUR WEB APPLICATION.
